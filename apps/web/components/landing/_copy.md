## Product Pillars

### Capture Without Friction

Share links, tweets, videos, images, receipts—anything—directly from your phone or Mac. No apps to learn, no folders to maintain.

## What the AI Does

### Classify

Understands what kind of thing you saved—article, image, video, receipt—without you saying a word.

### Extract

Pulls out the meaningful parts: prices from receipts, transcripts from videos, quotes from articles, automatically.

### Remember

Connects related items across everything you've ever saved. One thought leads to another, like your mind does.

### Retrieve via Chat

Ask "show me coffee shops I saved" or "what was that article about AI pricing?" Natural language, natural results.

## FAQ

### How much does it cost?

[CONFIRM: Free during beta. We'll announce paid plans when we launch publicly—likely tiered by storage and advanced AI features. Early users get grandfathered pricing.]

### Who can see what I share?

Only you. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never train on your content or sell access to it. Your inbox is yours alone.

### Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export all your saved items and chat history as JSON or CSV anytime. You own your data.
